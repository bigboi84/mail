Brighten Up Your Space —

Lighting and Lighting Design Tips

from Assembly

At Assembly, we understand that the right lighting can make all the difference. Especially when it comes to creating a warm, inviting space that reflects your unique style and personality.
That's why we wanted to share some lighting and lighting design tips with you to help you brighten up your home or office.

1. Layer Your Lighting

Instead of relying on one bright overhead light, consider layering your lighting by adding lamps, sconces, and other types of lighting fixtures. This will create a more dynamic and interesting lighting scheme.

2. Consider Color Temperature

Color temperature refers to the hue of the light, which can range from warm yellow to cool blue. Consider the mood you want to create and the colors in your space when selecting the color temperature of your bulbs.

3. Highlight Key Features

Use lighting to highlight key features in your space, such as artwork, architectural details, or plants. This will draw the eye and create visual interest.
